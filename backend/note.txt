ProInventory is an enterprise inventory, procurement, and point-of-sale platform that enables 
organizations to manage products, purchasing, stock, sales, users, and reporting across one or more 
locations with role-based access control.

Design for the backend:
the app is for any company that offers services to multiple people daily.
the backend of the app should be able to record sales, intake purchases, quantity in different metrics, selling price and cost price, able to provide receipt of sales, 
receipt of intake, show list of stock left, date of purchase, date of selling, date of anything done.
different staffs should have their access, name and password depending on their position in the company. the ceo and devs should have access to see everything,
some staffs like normal worker should see sales of their department and their inventory.
